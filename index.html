Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common dental questions

How often should I visit the dentist?

We recommend visiting the dentist every six months for a routine check-up and cleaning. However, some patients may need more frequent visits depending on their oral health needs.

Does teeth whitening damage enamel?

Professional teeth whitening performed by a dentist is safe and does not damage tooth enamel. We use controlled concentrations of whitening agents to ensure your teeth remain healthy while achieving a brighter smile.

Are dental X-rays safe?

Yes, dental X-rays are very safe. We use digital X-ray technology which reduces radiation exposure by up to 90% compared to traditional film X-rays. The amount of radiation is extremely small and equivalent to just a few days of natural background radiation.

What payment options do you accept?

We accept most major credit cards, cash, and personal checks. We also work with most dental insurance providers and offer flexible payment plans for more extensive treatments.

How can I overcome my fear of dentists?

We specialize in treating patients with dental anxiety. We offer sedation options, take time to explain procedures thoroughly, and create a calm environment. Many of our previously anxious patients now visit us without any fear.
